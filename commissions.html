    How to Order

    To order a commission, please contact me via email at fuzzyfoxf@gmail.com

    The cost of the commission will depend on the complexity and size of the piece. Here are some general pricing guidelines:

    • Sketch: $10-$20
    • Line Art: $20-$40
    • Flat Colors: $30-$60
    • Full Render: $50-$100+
    • Additional Characters: +$10-$30 each
    • Backgrounds: +$20-$50

    Please note that these are just general guidelines and the final price may vary based on the specific details of your commission.
