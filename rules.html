Please read the following rules before commissioning artwork:

Rules

  • No NSFW
  • No offensive or hateful content
  • No plagiarism or tracing
  • Respect the artist's style and boundaries
  • Provide clear and detailed references
  • Communicate openly and respectfully
  • Payment must be made in full before the artwork is delivered
  • Revisions are limited to two rounds unless otherwise agreed upon
  • All sales are final; no refunds
  • The artist retains the right to use the artwork for promotional purposes unless otherwise agreed upon
